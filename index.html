Technical Projects

High-Efficiency Solar UAV Wing Design

ANSYS Fluent XFLR5 Python Aerodynamics

Designed a high-efficiency wing for a solar-powered UAV to operate at low Reynolds numbers. Conducted a multi-stage screening process to select a hybrid airfoil configuration (S1223 Root, E387 Mid, S6063 Tip) . Optimized the wing geometry with a 4-degree twist to ensure root-first stall progression . Validated the design using ANSYS Fluent with the Transition SST turbulence model, identifying a divergence between XFLR5 potential flow and RANS CFD predictions regarding laminar separation bubbles .

NACA 2410 Airfoil CFD Study

CFD Fluid Mechanics Meshing

Conducted a computational fluid dynamics (CFD) study of the NACA 2410 airfoil to investigate aerodynamic behavior across laminar and turbulent regimes. Built refined meshes and varied angles of attack between -45° and 45° to analyze lift, drag, and stall onset. Validated simulations against experimental data, reinforcing proficiency in advanced CFD methodologies.

AI Lift & Drag Coefficient Model

Machine Learning Python Data Analysis
